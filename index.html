Choose Change

Awareness creates change, empowering different and productive alternatives to life’s problems.

Individuals

We offer a variety of therapeutic and treatment styles, and strive to individualize and customize therapy to fit each client.

Couples

We offer both individual and co-therapy for couples at all stages; dating, married, divorced, etc. We utilize multiple perspectives and are solution driven. Additionally our co-therapy team consists of one male and one female therapist to create balance within the therapy process.

Families

We offer a variety of approaches and work to create a unique therapy experience for each family. We are specially trained to work with families, big and small. We welcome working with children, teenagers and young adults.

Corporate Clients

We offer strategically developed solutions to corporate dysfunction and will create a custom experience for each corporation.
